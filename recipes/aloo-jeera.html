Aloo Jerra (Cumin Potatoes)

Description

Aloo Jeera is a simple and flavorful Indian side dish made with potatoes and cumin seeds. Itâ€™s quick to prepare and pairs well with rice or roti.

Ingredients

Here's a detailed recipe for Aloo Jeera, with all the ingredients listed in precise measurements:

  • 2 medium potatoes
  • 1 tablespoon cumin seeds
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander (optional for garnish)

Steps

  1. Peel and dice the potatoes into small cubes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and let them sizzle.
  3. Add the diced potatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt.
  4. Cook on medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the potatoes are tender and slightly crispy.
  5. Garnish with fresh coriander if desired and serve hot.

Enjoy your delicious Aloo Jeera!
