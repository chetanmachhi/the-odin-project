Tomato Rice

Description

Tomato Rice is a quick and easy dish featuring aromatic basmati rice cooked with tomatoes and spices. Itâ€™s a flavorful and satisfying meal on its own.

Ingredients

Here's a detailed recipe for Tomato Rice, with all the ingredients listed in precise measurements:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 medium tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Fresh coriander or mint leaves (optional for garnish)

Steps

  1. Wash and soak the rice for 15-20 minutes, then drain.
  2. Chop the tomatoes into small pieces.
  3. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and let them sizzle.
  4. Add the chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt.
  5. Cook until tomatoes soften and oil starts separating.
  6. Add the soaked rice and 2 cups of water.
  7. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat, cover, and simmer until the rice is cooked and water is absorbed.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander or mint leaves if desired and serve warm.

Enjoy your flavorful Tomato Rice!
